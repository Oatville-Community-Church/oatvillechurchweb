Plan Your First Visit

We're honored you're considering visiting {{name}}. Here's everything you need to feel comfortable and confident.

What to Expect

Services blend traditional hymns, organ, and some contemporary elements in a reverent but welcoming atmosphere. Come as you are – there's no dress code. You'll be greeted warmly, never spotlighted. Explore our ministries or review service times.

  • Parking available on site – arrive 10 minutes early
  • Friendly greeters & ushers if you need help finding a seat
  • Services last about 60–70 minutes
  • Prayer and community are central (opportunities to connect)

Service Times

  • Christian Education: 10:00 AM
  • Sunday Worship: 11:00 AM & 5:00 PM
  • Wednesday Prayer: 6:00 PM
  • YaWeh Study: 6:45 PM Wednesdays

Times shown in Central Time.

Children & Families

We love families. Nursery and children's ministry options are available during worship. You're welcome to keep your kids with you if you prefer; we support whatever helps your family worship best.

  • Safe, caring volunteers (screened)
  • Simple check-in process
  • Age‑appropriate teaching
  • Family seating always welcome

Before You Arrive

  1. Pick a service time
  2. Plan to arrive 10–15 minutes early
  3. Stop by to say hello – we’d love to meet you
  4. Grab a seat anywhere comfortable
  5. Complete a connect card if you'd like follow‑up

Common Questions

Do I need to dress up?

Wear what’s comfortable – jeans, casual, or traditional. All are welcome.

Will I be singled out?

No – you're our guest. We won't ask you to stand or introduce yourself publicly.

Is the building accessible?

Yes. Entrances and seating are accessible; let us know if you need assistance.
