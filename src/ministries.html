Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved in a ministry?

Start by attending our services and talking with ministry leaders. We'll help match your interests and gifts with our ministry needs.

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No experience required! We provide training and support for all volunteers. What matters most is a heart to serve.

What ministries are available for children?

We offer nursery care, children's church, Sunday school, and seasonal programs like Vacation Bible School for ages 0-11.

How often do ministries meet?

Most ministries meet weekly, with some meeting monthly or seasonally. Each ministry has its own schedule to accommodate different commitments.

Can visitors participate in ministries?

Absolutely! Visitors are welcome to participate in most ministry activities. Some leadership roles may require membership or completion of our volunteer process.
