We can't wait to meet you. Here's everything you need to feel at home from the moment you arrive.

Call Us Common Questions

What to Expect

Relax. You're among friends. We gather to worship Jesus, learn from Scripture, and encourage one another. Come as you are—casual or dressy.

Arrival & Parking

Greeters will welcome you. Accessible entry is available. Arrive 10–15 minutes early if you have kids to check in.

Service Style

Blended worship: traditional hymns + contemporary songs. Christ-centered preaching rooted in Scripture.

Length & Flow

Most services last about 60–70 minutes: music, prayer, teaching, response. Evening gathering offers a reflective tone.

Kids & Families

We love families. Our trained volunteers create a safe, fun, Christ-centered environment. Nursery and children's ministry available during services.

  • Secure kids check-in
  • Age-appropriate Bible teaching
  • Background-checked volunteers
  • Parent text-notification if needed
