About Our Church

Oatville Community Church is more than a place of worship - it's where tradition meets modern faith practices, creating a space for all seekers to connect and grow.

Our Story

For generations, Oatville Community Church has been a cornerstone of faith in Wichita. We've witnessed countless marriages, baptisms, and life celebrations, creating lasting memories and deep connections within our community.

Our atmosphere is warm and inviting, welcoming everyone with open arms. Whether you're a longtime member or visiting for the first time, you'll be embraced by a community dedicated to following God's path.

"It's more than a place of worship; it's where you feel the Holy Spirit among us. Each visit reaffirms my faith and leaves me inspired."

- Community Member
