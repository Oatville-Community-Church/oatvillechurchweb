Our Ministries

Serving God and people through worship, teaching, fellowship, and outreach. Planning a first visit? Plan your visit or learn about our service times.

Worship & Music

Blending traditional hymns, organ accompaniment, and occasional contemporary songs – Christ-centered and congregationally focused.

Children's Ministry

Safe, caring environments where kids learn Scripture, prayer, and foundational truths in age-appropriate ways.

Youth & Discipleship

Helping students grow in faith, biblical literacy, and resilient identity in Christ through study and mentoring.

Prayer & Study

Weekly prayer gatherings and deep-dive Bible study (YaWeh Study) to strengthen spiritual life and community intercession.

Care & Fellowship

Encouragement, visitation, and practical help for those in need – embodying Christ's love in action.

Outreach & Community

Local engagement, seasonal events, and service opportunities to share hope and serve our neighbors.
